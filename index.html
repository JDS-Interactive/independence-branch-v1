    What This Is / What This Isn’t

    What this is

    • A self-reflection tool for civic values
    • Deterministic scoring logic you can inspect
    • Private-by-default results generated on your device

    What this is not

    • Not a vote or referendum
    • Not a political endorsement
    • Not identity or citizenship verification
    • Not representative of public opinion

    Guiding principle: Clarity before authority. Transparency before influence. Integrity before scale.

    Transparency: Questionnaire logic and verification rules are deterministic and intended to be open-source. V1 stores no individual answers on a server.
